Frequently Asked Questions

Why does CourseBank need my photo?
As part of the verification process, you take a photo of both your face and a government-issued photo ID. Our authorization service confirms your identity by comparing the photo you take with the photo on your ID.
What does CourseBank do with this photo?
We directly send the submissions to our authorization service for review. Your photo and information are not saved or visible anywhere on CourseBank after the verification process is complete.
How long is the validity of my verification?
Your verification is good for 1 year upon approval of your verification submission. This means that you only need to re-submit your ID photos once this validity is expired.
